Bologna Cake

Description

What do you mean, "WTF?"

Ingredients

  • 2 packages cream cheese
  • 1 package Hidden Valley Ranch Dressing (1 ounce packet)
  • 12 ounces sliced bologna
  • 1 8 oz can of sharp cheddar aerosol cheese
  • 1 spring of cilantro for garnish
  • 1 package buttery crackers

Steps

  1. Mix ranch dressing into softened cream cheese. Set aside.
  2. Place a slice of bologna on a plate. Spread one tablespoon of cream cheese on top of the slice. Add a second slice of bologna. Continue until all bologna is used.
  3. Use remainder of the cream cheese to "frost" the cake. Decorate the cake with aerosol cheese. Top with cilantro sprig.
  4. Cut cake into wedges and serve on buttery crackers.
  5. Die alone.
